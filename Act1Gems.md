### Absolution
*Spell, Minion, Duration, Physical, Lightning, Area of Effect*

Casting Time: Action
Range: 60 feet
Area: 10-foot radius (Lesser), 15-foot radius (Standard), 20-foot radius (Greater)
Mana Cost: PRO/2, rounded up (Lesser), PRO (Standard), PRO + 3 (Greater)

A brilliant white-gold gem showing holy light striking down the wicked.

**Lesser**: As an action, you may spend PRO/2 mana (rounded up) to cast Absolution. You unleash divine judgment in a 10-foot radius within 60 feet, dealing PRO×1d4 bludgeoning damage and PRO×1d4 lightning damage to each creature in the area (Dexterity save for half, DC = spellcasting DC). Each affected creature gains a debuff for 1 round. If a non-unique enemy dies while debuffed, you consume the corpse to summon a Sentinel of Absolution for 10 rounds, or refresh an existing Sentinel's duration and hit points if you're at maximum. Maximum 3 Sentinels. Each Sentinel has AC 13, PRO×6 hit points, and attacks with +your spellcasting modifier dealing 1d8+PRO damage (50% bludgeoning, 50% lightning). You have a 25% chance to summon a Sentinel when hitting a rare or unique enemy.

**Standard**: As an action, you may spend PRO mana to cast Absolution. You unleash divine judgment in a 15-foot radius within 60 feet, dealing PRO×1d6 bludgeoning damage and PRO×1d6 lightning damage to each creature in the area (Dexterity save for half, DC = spellcasting DC). Each affected creature gains a debuff for 1 round. If a non-unique enemy dies while debuffed, you consume the corpse to summon a Sentinel of Absolution for 10 rounds, or refresh an existing Sentinel's duration and hit points if you're at maximum. Maximum 3 Sentinels. Each Sentinel has AC 14, PRO×8 hit points, and attacks with +your spellcasting modifier +1 dealing 1d10+PRO damage (50% bludgeoning, 50% lightning). You have a 25% chance to summon a Sentinel when hitting a rare or unique enemy.

**Greater**: As an action, you may spend PRO + 3 mana to cast Absolution. You unleash divine judgment in a 20-foot radius within 60 feet, dealing PRO×1d8 bludgeoning damage and PRO×1d8 lightning damage to each creature in the area (Dexterity save for half, DC = spellcasting DC). Each affected creature gains a debuff for 2 rounds. If a non-unique enemy dies while debuffed, you consume the corpse to summon a Sentinel of Absolution for 10 rounds, or refresh an existing Sentinel's duration and hit points if you're at maximum. Maximum 3 Sentinels. Each Sentinel has AC 15, PRO×10 hit points, and attacks with +your spellcasting modifier +2 dealing 1d12+PRO damage (50% bludgeoning, 50% lightning). You have a 25% chance to summon a Sentinel when hitting a rare or unique enemy. Sentinels deal 38% more damage and attack 38% faster.

### Animate Weapon
*Duration, Minion, Spell, Physical*

Casting Time: Action
Range: 30 feet
Mana Cost: PRO/2, rounded up (Lesser), PRO (Standard), PRO + 3 (Greater)

A shimmering silver gem showing spectral weapons coming to life.

**Lesser**: As an action, you may spend PRO/2 mana (rounded up) to animate a weapon within 30 feet. The weapon becomes a flying animated minion that fights for you. You can animate weapons up to your current level. The animated weapon has AC 12, PRO×5 hit points, lasts for 40 rounds, and makes melee attacks (+your spellcasting modifier to hit) dealing the weapon's normal damage + PRO×1d4 physical damage. Maximum 5 animated weapons. You cannot animate unidentified or magical weapons with special properties.

**Standard**: As an action, you may spend PRO mana to animate a weapon within 30 feet. The weapon becomes a flying animated minion that fights for you. You can animate weapons up to your current level. The animated weapon has AC 13, PRO×7 hit points, lasts for 40 rounds, and makes melee attacks (+your spellcasting modifier +1 to hit) dealing the weapon's normal damage + PRO×1d6 physical damage. The weapons attack 15% faster and deal 15% more damage. Maximum 10 animated weapons. You cannot animate unidentified or magical weapons with special properties.

**Greater**: As an action, you may spend PRO + 3 mana to animate a weapon within 30 feet. The weapon becomes a flying animated minion that fights for you. You can animate weapons up to your current level. The animated weapon has AC 14, PRO×9 hit points, lasts for 40 rounds, and makes melee attacks (+your spellcasting modifier +2 to hit) dealing the weapon's normal damage + PRO×1d8 physical damage. The weapons attack 38% faster and deal 38% more damage. Maximum 14 animated weapons. You cannot animate unidentified or magical weapons with special properties.

### Arc
*Spell, Chaining, Lightning*

Casting Time: Action
Range: 60 feet
Mana Cost: PRO/2, rounded up (Lesser), PRO (Standard), PRO + 3 (Greater)

A brilliant blue gem with arcing lightning that chains between multiple points.

**Lesser**: As an action, you may spend PRO/2 mana (rounded up) to cast Arc. An arc of lightning leaps from you to a target within 60 feet, then chains to up to 4 additional enemies within 30 feet of each previous target (cannot chain back to already-hit enemies immediately). Each enemy hit takes PRO×1d4 lightning damage (no save required). Each time the arc chains, it deals 15% more damage than the previous hit.

**Standard**: As an action, you may spend PRO mana to cast Arc. An arc of lightning leaps from you to a target within 60 feet, then chains to up to 5 additional enemies within 30 feet of each previous target (cannot chain back to already-hit enemies immediately). Each enemy hit takes PRO×1d6 lightning damage (no save required). Each time the arc chains, it deals 15% more damage than the previous hit. This attack has +5 to Shock Threshold.

**Greater**: As an action, you may spend PRO + 3 mana to cast Arc. An arc of lightning leaps from you to a target within 60 feet, then chains to up to 7 additional enemies within 30 feet of each previous target (cannot chain back to already-hit enemies immediately). Each enemy hit takes PRO×1d8 lightning damage (no save required). Each time the arc chains, it deals 15% more damage than the previous hit. This attack has +6 to Shock Threshold. Additionally, each chain creates a secondary arc that hits one additional enemy not already hit by the main arc.

### Barrage
*Attack, Projectile, Bow*

Casting Time: Action (after short preparation)
Range: Normal weapon range
Mana Cost: Lesser (1), Standard (2), Greater (3)

A green gem showing multiple arrows firing in rapid succession.

**Lesser**: As an action, you may spend 1 mana to use Barrage with a bow or crossbow. After a brief moment of preparation, you fire 5 projectiles rapidly in sequence at a single target. Each projectile has a small random spread. Make 6 separate attack rolls. Each projectile that hits deals your weapon damage × 50% + 1d4 damage. This attack requires you to remain stationary during the preparation.

**Greater**: As an action, you may spend 3 mana to use Barrage with a bow or crossbow. After a brief moment of preparation, you fire 7 projectiles rapidly in sequence at a single target. Each projectile has a small random spread. Make 7 separate attack rolls. Each projectile that hits deals your weapon damage × 55% + 1d6 damage. This attack requires you to remain stationary during the preparation. You can move up to 5 feet during the barrage without interrupting it.

**Greater**: As an action, you may spend PRO + 3 mana to throw a Bear Trap at a location within 30 feet. The trap lasts for 4 rounds. When a creature moves within 5 feet of the trap, it triggers, dealing PRO×1d8 bludgeoning damage (this damage is not affected by spell damage modifiers and cannot be reflected). The creature must succeed on a Strength saving throw (DC = spellcasting DC) or become locked in place (restrained) for up to 4 rounds (based on damage dealt - more damage = longer duration). After the restrain ends, the creature has a debuff for 4 rounds causing them to take 25% more damage from traps and mines.

**Greater**: When making an attack with a dagger, claw, or one-handed sword, you may spend 3 mana to throw a Blade Trap at a location within 30 feet. The trap lasts for 4 rounds. When triggered, the trap creates two spectral copies of your equipped weapon that spin in a 20-foot radius, rotating 5 times over the trap's duration. Each rotation, make an attack roll against each creature in the radius. On a hit, deal your weapon damage × 85% + 1d6.
