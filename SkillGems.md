# Skill Gems

## Active Skill Gems

### Blight
*Skill Gem, Spell, Channelling, Duration, Area of Affect*

This is a blue gem with gold gilding.

**Lesser**: As an action, you may pay mana equal to a third of your proficiency (1-2) to channel blight. If you channelled blight as an action, you may also channel it as a bonus action. You release a wave of black withering energy in front of you. Each enemy in a 10' cone recieves a stack of blight. If they did not previously have any blight, they must pass a Constitution Saving throw with DC = 8 + INT or their movement is reduced by 25', to a minimum of 5' for one round. At the start of their turn, a creature with blight takes necrotic damage equal to your proficiency modifier for each stack of blight on them. Each stack lasts for 4 rounds and there can only be 10 stacks at max.

**Greater**: As an action, you may pay mana equal to a half of your proficiency (1-3) to channel blight. If you channelled blight as an action, you may also channel it as a bonus action. You release a wave of black withering energy in front of you. Each enemy in a 15' cone recieves a stack of blight. If they did not previously have any blight, they must pass a Constitution Saving throw with DC = 8 + PRO + INT or their movement is reduced by 35', to a minimum of 5' for one round. At the start of their turn, a creature with blight takes necrotic damage equal to your proficiency modifier for each stack of blight on them. Each stack lasts for 5 rounds and there can only be 10 stacks at max.

### Burning Arrow
*Skill Gem, Attack, Ranged, Ignite, Projectile*

This is a green gem with gold gilding.

**Lesser**: When making a ranged attack with a bow or crossbow, you may spend 1 mana to use Burning Arrow. Burning Arrow deals an extra 1d4 fire damage and has +1 to ignite threshold. Targets ignited by Burning Arrow take 1d6 fire damage from ignite.

**Greater**: When making a ranged attack with a bow or crossbow, you may spend 2 mana to use Burning Arrow. Burning Arrow dealls an extra 1d8 fire damage and has +2 to ignite threshold. Targets ignited by Burning Arrow take 1d8 fire damage from ignite.

### Caustic Arrow
*Skill Gem, Attack, Ranged, Projectile, Area of Affect*

This is a green gem with gold gilding.

**Lesser**: When making a ranged attack with a bow or crossbow, you may spend 1 mana to use Caustic Arrow. Wherever the arrow lands a cloud of acidic gas spreads in a circle with 5' radius and lasts 1 round. Any creature within the cloud must pass a Dexterity Saving Throw with DC = 8 + DEX or take (1d6)xPRO acid damage. Any creature that ends its turn in the cloud takes (1d6)xPRO acid damage. Increases to projectile damage increase the cloud damage.

**Greater**: When making a ranged attack with a bow or crossbow, you may spend 2 mana to use Caustic Arrow. Wherever the arrow lands a cloud of acidic gas spreads in a circle with 5' radius and lasts 2 rounds. Any creature within the cloud must pass a Dexterity Saving Throw with DC = 8 + PRO + DEX or take (1d8)xPRO acid damage. Any creature that ends its turn in the cloud takes (1d8)xPRO acid damage. Increases to projectile damage increase the cloud damage.

### Cleave
*Skill Gem, Attack, Melee, Area of Affect*

This is a red gem with gold gilding.

**Lesser**: When making a melee attack with a slashing weapon, you may spend 1 mana to use Cleave. You may attack up to 3 enemies that are in front of you. If you are dualwielding melee weapons, roll for both weapons on hit.

**Greater**: When making a melee attack with a slashing weapon, you may spend 2 mana to use Cleave. You may attack up to 5 enemies that are in front of you and gain 5' to your reach. If you are dualwielding melee weapons, roll for both weapons on hit.

### Double Strike
*Skill Gem, Attack, Melee*

This is a green gem with gold gilding.

**Lesser**: When making a melee attack, you may spend 1 mana to make a Double Strike. Make two attacks with disadvantage on the damage roll. If the target is bleeding, roll an additional 1d4 for the damage with Double Strike.

**Greater**: When making an attack, you may spend 1 mana to make a Double Strike. Make two attacks. If the target is bleeding, roll an additional 1d8 for the damage with Double Strike.

### Ethereal Knives
*Skill Gem, Spell, Projectile, Area of Affect*

This is a green gem with gold gilding.

**Lesser**: As an action, you may spend mana equal to half your proficiency modifier (1-3) to cast Ethereal Knives. You conjure 6+PRO ethereal knives, which fly out in a 15' cone in front of you. You decide what targets the knives hit, up to a maximum of three per target. Each target takes 1d4 piercing damage per knife that hits them or half damage on a successful DC = 8 + DEX Dexterity saving throw.

**Greater**: As an action, you may spend mana equal to your proficiency modifier (2-6) to cast Ethereal Knives. You fire 6+PRO ethereal knives in a 20' cone in front of you. You decide what targets the knives hit, up to a maximum of three per target. Each target takes 1d6 piercing damage per knife that hits them or half damage on a successful DC = 8 + PRO + DEX Dexterity saving throw.

**Greater**: As an action, you can spend mana equal to your proficiency modifier (2-6) to cast Firestrike. Make a spell attack with +6 to ignite Threshold against a target within 60' and on hit deal (1d6)xPRO fire damage. A creature ignited by Firestrike takes 1d8 fire damage from ignite.

### Freezing Pulse

This is a blue gem with gold gilding.

Each enemy in a 20' line takes (1d4)xPRO cold damage or half damage on a successful DC = 8 + INT Dexterity saving throw with +4 to its Chill Threshold. **Greater**:  As an action, you can spend mana equal to your proficiency modifier (2-6) to cast Freezing Pulse. Each enemy in a 25' line takes (1d6)xPRO cold damage or half damage on a successful DC = 8 + PRO + INT Dexterity with +6 to its Chill Threshold. For each 5' in the distance to a target, the DC decreases by 1 and the damage decreases by 2.

### Frost Blades

This is a green gem with gold gilding.

On hit, 4 icy projectiles fly out in a cone behind the target, hitting seperate enemies. Each enemy hit by one of these projectiles takes cold damage equal to the initial strike.

As you strike, your weapon gives off an icy flash, giving you +10' to reach and adding 1d6 as cold damage to your attack. On hit, 4 icy projectiles fly out in a cone behind the target, hitting seperate enemies. Each enemy hit by one of these projectiles takes cold damage equal to the initial strike.

You create an orb of swirling ice that moves 10' now and again on initiative counts 20 and 10, piercing all targets. Each creature it passes through must make a DC = 8 + PRO + INT Dexterity saving throw, taking (1d6)xPRO cold damage on a failed saving throw, or half damage on a successful saving throw. After the orb has travelled 120', it disappears.

### Glacial Hammer
*Skill Gem, Attack, Melee, Cold*

**Lesser**: When making a melee attack with a bludgeoning weapon, you may spend 1 mana to use Glacial Hammer. Your weapon becomes encased in ice. When using Glacial Hammer, your bludgeoning damage becomes cold damage and your attack has +4 to Chill Threshold. Chill caused by Glacial Hammer lasts +1 round. If the target is already frozen on hit, deal an additional 3 force damage.

**Greater**: When making a melee attack with a bludgeoning weapon, you may spend 2 mana to use Glacial Hammer. When you use Glacial Hammer, your weapon becomes encased in ice, and your bludgeoning damage becomes cold damage and your attack deals 1d6 extra damage and has +4 to Chill Threshold. Chill caused by Glacial Hammer lasts +1 round. If the target is already frozen on hit, deal an additional 6 force damage.

### Ground Slam
*Skill Gem, Attack, Melee, Area of Affect*

This is a red gem with gold gilding.

**Lesser**: When making a melee attack with a bludgeoning weapon, you may spend 1 mana to use Ground Slam. You hit the ground with your weapon creating a wave that travels out in a 15' cone. Each enemy in the cone takes bludgeoning damage equal to your weapon die + 1d4 or half damage on a successfuly DC = 8 + PRO + STR Dexterity saving throw. This skill has +5 to Stun Threshold. **Greater**: When making a melee attack with a bludgeoning weapon, you may spend 2 mana to use Ground Slam. You hit the ground with your weapon creating a wave that travels out in a 20' cone. Each enemy in the cone takes bludgeoning damage equal to twice your weapon die (roll two dice) and Stun Threshold decrease by 1 for every 5' between you and them.

### Lightning Tendrils
*Skill Gem, Spell, Area of Affect, Lightning, Channelling*

This is a blue gem with gold gilding.

**Lesser**: As an action, you may pay mana equal to 1/3 of your proficiency modifier (1-2) to channel Lightning Tendrils. Each enemy within a 5' semicircle makes a DEX saving throw with DC = 8 + INT. They take 1d12x(PRO/2) lightning damage on a fail, or half damage on a success. Every 4th cast while channeling does 1.5x as much damage. If you cast Lightning Tendrils as an action, you may cast it again in the same turn as a bonus action.

**Greater**: As an action, you may pay mana equal to 1/2 of your proficiency modifier (1-3) to channel Lightning Tendrils. Each enemy within a 10' semicircle makes a DEX saving throw with DC = 8 + PRO + INT. They take 1d12xPRO lightning damage on a fail, or half damage on a success. Every 4th cast while channeling does 1.5x as much damage. If you cast Lightning Tendrils as an action, you may cast it again in the same turn as a bonus action.

Enemies that are within the explosion take (1d6+1)xPRO fire damage or half damage on a successful DC = 8 + PRO + INT Dexterity Saving Throw.

## Support Skill Gems
### Arcane Surge
*Support Gem, Spell, Duration, Duration*

This is a blue gem with gold gilding.

**Lesser**: When using a spell skill, you may spend 1 mana to give the skill Heightening. Whenever you spend a total of 6 mana on a Heightened skill, take a d6 as an Arcane Surge die. You may only have 1 Arcane Surge die at a time. At any time, you may spend the Arcane Surge die to add 5' to your movement or to roll it and add the result to one of the following: Spell attacks, spell damage, and WIS and INT ability checks.

---

**Greater**: When using a spell skill, you may spend 2 mana to give the skill Heightening. Whenever you spend a total of 18 mana on a Heightened skill, take a d8 as an Arcane Surge die. You may only have 2 Arcane Surge die at a time. At any time, you may spend the Arcane Surge die to add 5' to your movement or to roll it and add the result to one of the following: Spell attacks, spell damage, and WIS and INT ability checks.

### Chance to Bleed
*Support Gem, Attack*

This is a red gem with gold gilding.

**Lesser**: When using an attack skill that deals slashing or piercing damage, you may spend 1 mana to give the skill Chance to Bleed. On hit with Chance to Bleed, the attack deals +1 damage and has +5 to its Bleed Threshold.

**Greater**: When using an attack skill, you may spend 2 mana to give the skill Chance to Bleed. On hit with Chance to Bleed, the attack deals +2 damage and has +5 to its Bleed Threshold. Bleeding dealt by Chance to Bleed deals 1 additional damage per round.

### Elemental Proliferation
*Support Gem, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Support, Area of Affect*

This is a blue gem with gold gilding.

**Lesser**: When using a skill that deals elemental damage, you may spend 1 mana to give the skill Elemental Proliferation. The skill supported by Elemental Proliferation has +2 to its Chill, Ignite, and Shock Thresholds. While a target is chilled, frozen, ignited, or shocked from the supported skill, whenever an enemy begins its turn or moves within 5' of the target, the ailment spreads to him. A enemy can avoid being frozen in this way by making a Constitutional saving throw with DC = 8 + PRO + INT.

**Greater**: When using a skill that deals elemental damage, you may spend 2 mana to give the skill Elemental Proliferation. The skill supported by Elemental Proliferation has +2 to its Chill, Ignite, and Shock Thresholds. While a target is chilled, frozen, ignited, or shocked from the supported skill, whenever an enemy begins its turn or moves within 10' of the target, the ailment spreads to him. A enemy can avoid being frozen in this way by making a Constitutional saving throw with DC = 8 + PRO + INT.

### Onslaught
*Support Gem*

This is a green gem with gold gilding.

**Lesser**: When using a skill, you may spend 1 mana to give the skill Onslaught. If you kill a creature with an Onslaught skill, roll a d20. On a 16+, you gain the Onslaught buff for 2 rounds. While you have the Onslaught buff, you may use your bonus action to attack or cast spells that normally require an action (this includes spells from skill gems) and add 10' to your movement.

**Greater**: When using a skill, you may spend 2 mana to give the skill Onslaught. If you kill a creature with an Onslaught skill, roll a d20. On a 11+, you gain the Onslaught buff for 2 rounds. While you have the Onslaught buff, you may make a free attack during your turn, use your bonus action to cast spells that normally require an action (this includes spells from skill gems) and add 10' to your movement.

### Pierce

**Greater**: When using a projectile skill, you may spend 2 mana to give the skill Pierce. A skill with pierce will pass through 2 enemies without stopping. A skill that chains cannot pierce.

### Poison

This is a green gem with gold gilding.

**Greater**: When using a skill that deals physical or necrotic damage, you may spend 2 mana to give the skill Poison. The attack has +6 to its Inflict Threshold, and Inflicted caused by this attack deal 2 bonus damage per round.

### Ruthless
*Support Gem, Melee, Attack*

This is a red gem with gold gilding.

**Lesser**: When using a melee attack skill, you may spend 1 mana to make the attack ruthless. You have disadvantage on the attack, and roll an extra die for the damage. If this skill causes bleeding, the bleeding damage is increased by 3.

**Greater**: When using a melee attack skill, you may spend 1 mana to make the attack ruthless. You have disadvantage on the attack, and roll 2 extra die for the damage. If this skill causes bleeding, the bleeding damage is increased by 3.
