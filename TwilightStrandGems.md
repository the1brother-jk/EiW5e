## Active Skill Gems

### Burning Arrow
*Attack, Projectile, Fire, Bow*

Casting Time: When making a ranged attack with a bow or crossbow
Range: Normal weapon range
Mana Cost: Lesser (PRO/2, rounded up), Standard (PRO), Greater (PRO+3)

This is a green gem with gold gilding. When held or socketed, the gem glows with flickering orange light.

**Lesser**: When making a ranged attack with a bow or crossbow, you may spend mana equal to PRO/2 (rounded up) to use Burning Arrow. Your physical damage is converted to fire damage. On hit, the attack deals an additional PRO×(1d4) fire damage. This attack has +4 to Ignite Threshold.

**Standard**: When making a ranged attack with a bow or crossbow, you may spend mana equal to PRO to use Burning Arrow. Your physical damage is converted to fire damage. On hit, the attack deals an additional PRO×(1d6) fire damage. This attack has +5 to Ignite Threshold.

**Greater**: When making a ranged attack with a bow or crossbow, you may spend mana equal to PRO+3 to use Burning Arrow. Your physical damage is converted to fire damage. On hit, the attack deals an additional PRO×(1d8) fire damage. This attack has +6 to Ignite Threshold

### Double Strike
*Attack, Melee, Strike, Physical*

Casting Time: When making a melee attack
Range: Melee weapon reach
Mana Cost: Lesser (PRO/2, rounded up), Standard (PRO), Greater (PRO+3)

This is a green gem with gold gilding. The gem pulses with a steady rhythm, like a heartbeat.

**Lesser**: When making a melee attack, you may spend mana equal to PRO/2 (rounded up) to use Double Strike. You make two rapid strikes against the same target. Roll to hit twice. Each hit that succeeds deals your weapon damage. This attack has +4 to Bleed Threshold.

**Standard**: When making a melee attack, you may spend mana equal to PRO to use Double Strike. You make two rapid strikes against the same target. Roll to hit twice. Each hit that succeeds deals your weapon damage + 1d4. This attack has +5 to Bleed Threshold.

**Greater**: When making a melee attack, you may spend mana equal to PRO+3 to use Double Strike. You make two rapid strikes against the same target. Roll to hit twice. Each hit that succeeds deals your weapon damage + 1d6. This attack has +6 to Bleed Threshold. If the target is already bleeding, each strike deals an additional PRO damage.

### Firestrike
*Projectile, Spell, Area of Effect, Fire*

Casting Time: As an action
Range: 60 feet (Lesser), 80 feet (Standard), 100 feet (Greater)
Area: 5-foot radius (Lesser), 10-foot radius (Standard), 15-foot radius (Greater)
Mana Cost: Lesser (PRO/2, rounded up), Standard (PRO), Greater (PRO+3)

This is a blue gem with gold gilding. The gem radiates warmth and contains what appears to be a tiny dancing flame.

**Lesser**: As an action, you may spend mana equal to PRO/2 (rounded up) to cast Firestrike. You hurl a ball of fire at a point you can see within 60 feet. The fire explodes in a 5-foot radius. Each creature in the area must make a Dexterity saving throw with DC equal to your spellcasting DC, taking PRO×(1d4) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. This attack has +4 to Ignite Threshold for all affected creatures.

**Standard**: As an action, you may spend mana equal to PRO to cast Firestrike. You hurl a ball of fire at a point you can see within 80 feet. The fire explodes in a 10-foot radius. Each creature in the area must make a Dexterity saving throw with DC equal to your spellcasting DC, taking PRO×(1d6) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. This attack has +5 to Ignite Threshold for all affected creatures.

**Greater**: As an action, you may spend mana equal to PRO+3 to cast Firestrike. You hurl a ball of fire at a point you can see within 100 feet. The fire explodes in a 15-foot radius. Each creature in the area must make a Dexterity saving throw with DC equal to your spellcasting DC, taking PRO×(1d8) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. This attack has +6 to Ignite Threshold for all affected creatures.

### Glacial Hammer
*Attack, Melee, Strike, Cold*

Casting Time: When making a melee attack with a bludgeoning weapon
Range: Melee weapon reach
Mana Cost: Lesser (PRO/2, rounded up), Standard (PRO), Greater (PRO+3)

This is a red gem with gold gilding. Frost crystals seem to form and melt on its surface continuously.

**Lesser**: When making a melee attack with a bludgeoning weapon, you may spend mana equal to PRO/2 (rounded up) to use Glacial Hammer. Your weapon becomes encased in ice. Your physical damage is converted to cold damage and the attack deals an additional PRO×(1d4) cold damage. This attack has +4 to Chill Threshold. Cold ailments caused by this attack last 1 additional round. If the target is already frozen on hit, deal an additional PRO force damage.

**Standard**: When making a melee attack with a bludgeoning weapon, you may spend mana equal to PRO to use Glacial Hammer. Your weapon becomes encased in ice. Your physical damage is converted to cold damage and the attack deals an additional PRO×(1d6) cold damage. This attack has +5 to Chill Threshold. Cold ailments caused by this attack last 1 additional rounds. If the target is already frozen on hit, deal an additional 2*PRO force damage.

**Greater**: When making a melee attack with a bludgeoning weapon, you may spend mana equal to PRO+3 to use Glacial Hammer. Your weapon becomes encased in ice. Your physical damage is converted to cold damage and the attack deals an additional PRO×(1d8) cold damage. This attack has +6 to Chill Threshold. Cold ailments caused by this attack last 2 additional rounds. If the target is already frozen on hit, deal an additional 3*PRO force damage.

### Heavy Strike
*Attack, Melee, Strike*

Casting Time: When making a melee attack
Range: Melee weapon reach
Mana Cost: Lesser (PRO/2, rounded up), Standard (PRO), Greater (PRO+3)

This is a red gem with gold gilding. The gem feels heavier than it should be for its size.

**Lesser**: When making a melee attack with a mace, sceptre, axe, sword, or staff, you may spend mana equal to PRO/2 (rounded up) to use Heavy Strike. This attack has +4 to Stun Threshold. On hit, the attack deals an additional PRO×(1d4) damage. Roll 1d20; on a 17 or higher, the damage dealt by this attack is doubled.

**Standard**: When making a melee attack with a mace, sceptre, axe, sword, or staff, you may spend mana equal to PRO to use Heavy Strike. This attack has +5 to Stun Threshold. On hit, the attack deals an additional PRO×(1d6) damage. Roll 1d20; on a 16 or higher, the damage dealt by this attack is doubled.

**Greater**: When making a melee attack with a mace, sceptre, axe, sword, or staff, you may spend mana equal to PRO+3 to use Heavy Strike. This attack has +6 to Stun Threshold. On hit, the attack deals an additional PRO×(1d8) damage and knocks the target back 10 feet. Roll 1d20; on a 15 or higher, the damage dealt by this attack is doubled.

### Spectral Throw
*Attack, Projectile*

Casting Time: When making an attack with a melee weapon
Range: 30 feet (Lesser/Standard), 40 feet (Greater)
Mana Cost: Lesser (PRO/2, rounded up), Standard (PRO), Greater (PRO+3)

This is a green gem with gold gilding. Ghostly images of weapons seem to swirl within the gem.

**Lesser**: When making an attack with a melee weapon, you may spend mana equal to PRO/2 (rounded up) to use Spectral Throw. A spectral copy of your weapon flies out in a straight line up to 30 feet. Roll to hit each creature it passes through. At the end of the round the spectral weapon will return to you. Roll to hit each creature it passes through. On hit, the spectral weapon deals damage equal to your weapon damage.

**Standard**: When making an attack with a melee weapon, you may spend mana equal to PRO to use Spectral Throw. A spectral copy of your weapon flies out in a straight line up to 30 feet. Roll to hit each creature it passes through. At the end of the round the spectral weapon will return to you. Roll to hit each creature it passes through. On hit, the spectral weapon deals damage equal to your weapon damage + 1d4.

**Greater**: When making an attack with a melee weapon, you may spend mana equal to PRO+3 to use Spectral Throw. A spectral copy of your weapon flies out in a straight line up to 40 feet. Roll to hit each creature it passes through. At the end of the round the spectral weapon will return to you. Roll to hit each creature it passes through. On hit, the spectral weapon deals damage equal to your weapon damage + 1d6.

### Viper Strike
*Attack, Duration, Melee, Strike, Chaos*

Casting Time: When making a melee attack
Range: Melee weapon reach
Mana Cost: Lesser (PRO/2, rounded up), Standard (PRO), Greater (PRO+3)

This is a green gem with gold gilding. The gem has a sickly green tint and seems to pulse with dark energy.

**Lesser**: When making a melee attack with a claw, dagger, or sword, you may spend mana equal to PRO/2 (rounded up) to use Viper Strike. 60% of your physical damage is converted to necrotic damage. On hit, the attack deals an additional PRO×(1d4) necrotic damage. This attack has +6 to Inflict Threshold. Inflicted caused by this attack lasts for 4 rounds.

**Standard**: When making a melee attack with a claw, dagger, or sword, you may spend mana equal to PRO to use Viper Strike. 60% of your physical damage is converted to necrotic damage. On hit, the attack deals an additional PRO×(1d6) necrotic damage. This attack has +8 to Inflict Threshold. Inflicted caused by this attack lasts for 6 rounds and deals 1 additional damage per round.

**Greater**: When making a melee attack with a claw, dagger, or sword, you may spend mana equal to PRO+3 to use Viper Strike. 60% of your physical damage is converted to necrotic damage. On hit, the attack deals an additional PRO×(1d8) necrotic damage. This attack has +10 to Inflict Threshold. Inflicted caused by this attack lasts for 8 rounds and deals 2 additional damage per round. If dual wielding, you may make an attack with your off-hand weapon as part of the same attack action, but this second strike deals 20% less damage.

## Support Skill Gems

### Arcane Surge
*Arcane, Support, Spell, Duration*

This is a blue gem with gold gilding. Electric blue light crackles across its surface intermittently.

Support gems modify the cost and effect of active skill gems. When supporting a Lesser skill, add +1 to the mana cost. When supporting a Standard skill, add +2 to the mana cost. When supporting a Greater skill, add +3 to the mana cost.

When using a spell skill, you may add the Arcane Surge support to that skill, increasing its mana cost as described above. The supported skill gains the following effect:

Track the total mana you spend on this supported skill. When you have spent a total of 6×PRO mana (cumulative across multiple casts), you gain the Arcane Surge buff for 4 rounds. While you have Arcane Surge:
- Your casting time for spells is reduced by one step (action to bonus action, or you may cast an additional spell as a bonus action if you cast a spell as an action).
- You regain 1 mana at the start of each of your turns.
- The supported spell deals additional damage equal to PRO for each die rolled.

The mana tracking resets after you gain the Arcane Surge buff.

### Chance to Bleed
*Attack, Physical, Support*

This is a red gem with gold gilding. Dark red liquid seems to flow through channels in the gem.

Support gems modify the cost and effect of active skill gems. When supporting a Lesser skill, add +1 to the mana cost. When supporting a Standard skill, add +2 to the mana cost. When supporting a Greater skill, add +3 to the mana cost.

When using an attack skill that deals physical damage, you may add the Chance to Bleed support to that skill, increasing its mana cost as described above. The supported skill gains the following effects:

- The attack has +4 to Bleed Threshold (Lesser skill), +5 to Bleed Threshold (Standard skill), or +6 to Bleed Threshold (Greater skill)
- Bleeding caused by this attack deals additional damage per round equal to PRO/2 (rounded up)

### Chance to Poison
*Attack, Support, Chaos*

This is a green gem with gold gilding. Wisps of green vapor seem to swirl within the gem.

Support gems modify the cost and effect of active skill gems. When supporting a Lesser skill, add +1 to the mana cost. When supporting a Standard skill, add +2 to the mana cost. When supporting a Greater skill, add +3 to the mana cost.

When using an attack skill that deals physical or necrotic damage, you may add the Chance to Poison support to that skill, increasing its mana cost as described above. The supported skill gains the following effects:

- The attack has +4 to Inflict Threshold (Lesser skill), +5 to Inflict Threshold (Standard skill), or +6 to Inflict Threshold (Greater skill)
- Inflicted caused by this attack deals additional damage per round equal to PRO/2 (rounded up)

### Elemental Proliferation
*Cold, Fire, Lightning, Support, Area of Effect*

This is a blue gem with gold gilding. The gem shifts between icy blue, fiery orange, and electric yellow hues.

Support gems modify the cost and effect of active skill gems. When supporting a Lesser skill, add +1 to the mana cost. When supporting a Standard skill, add +2 to the mana cost. When supporting a Greater skill, add +3 to the mana cost.

When using a skill that deals cold, fire, or lightning damage, you may add the Elemental Proliferation support to that skill, increasing its mana cost as described above. The supported skill gains the following effects:

- The attack has +3 to Chill, Ignite, and Shock Thresholds
- Elemental ailments (Chill, Frozen, Ignite, Shock) caused by the supported skill spread to other enemies within 5 feet (Lesser skill), 10 feet (Standard skill), or 15 feet (Greater skill) of the afflicted target. When an enemy starts their turn within this radius of an afflicted creature, they must make a Constitution saving throw with DC equal to your spellcasting DC or be afflicted with the same ailment for the remaining duration
- Duration of elemental ailments caused by this skill is increased by 1 round

### Momentum
*Attack, Support, Duration*

This is a red gem with gold gilding. The gem seems to vibrate with barely contained energy.

Support gems modify the cost and effect of active skill gems. When supporting a Lesser skill, add +1 to the mana cost. When supporting a Standard skill, add +2 to the mana cost. When supporting a Greater skill, add +3 to the mana cost.

When using an attack skill (that is not channeling), you may add the Momentum support to that skill, increasing its mana cost as described above. The supported skill gains the following effects:

Each time you use the supported skill without having moved since your last turn, you gain 1 Momentum stack. You can have a maximum of 3 Momentum stacks (Lesser skill), 4 Momentum stacks (Standard skill), or 5 Momentum stacks (Greater skill). If you move more than 5 feet on your turn, you lose all Momentum stacks.

For each Momentum stack you have:
- You gain +5 feet of movement speed

When you reach maximum Momentum stacks, you lose all stacks and gain the Swiftness buff for 1 round. While you have Swiftness:
- You gain movement speed equal to 10 feet × the number of Momentum stacks lost
- You may make one additional attack as part of your Attack action

### Prismatic Burst
*Support, Spell, Area of Effect, Fire, Cold, Lightning, Trigger*

This is a blue gem with gold gilding. Three colors dance within the gem, never quite mixing.

Support gems modify the cost and effect of active skill gems. When supporting a Lesser skill, add +1 to the mana cost. When supporting a Standard skill, add +2 to the mana cost. When supporting a Greater skill, add +3 to the mana cost.

When using an attack skill, you may add the Prismatic Burst support to that skill, increasing its mana cost as described above. The supported skill gains the following effects:

On hit with the supported attack, you trigger a Prismatic Burst. Before making the attack, choose one damage type: fire, cold, or lightning. The attack can only deal damage of that damage type, and each creature within 5 feet (Lesser skill), 10 feet (Standard skill), or 15 feet (Greater skill) of the target must make a Dexterity saving throw with DC equal to your spellcasting DC. On a failed save, they take PRO×(1d4) (Lesser), PRO×(1d6) (Standard), or PRO×(1d8) (Greater) damage of the chosen type, or half as much on a successful save.

Once you have triggered a prismatic burst, you cannot do so again until it is recharged. At the start of your turn, roll 1d6. On a 6, the ability is recharged.

### Ruthless
*Slam, Support, Melee, Attack, Strike*

This is a red gem with gold gilding. The gem feels unnaturally heavy and radiates an aura of devastating force.

Support gems modify the cost and effect of active skill gems. When supporting a Lesser skill, add +1 to the mana cost. When supporting a Standard skill, add +2 to the mana cost. When supporting a Greater skill, add +3 to the mana cost.

When using a melee strike or slam attack skill, you may add the Ruthless support to that skill, increasing its mana cost as described above. The supported skill gains the following effects:

Track the number of times you use the supported skill. Every third use of the supported skill is a Ruthless Blow. When making a Ruthless Blow:
- The attack deals double damage
- If the attack causes an ailment (Bleed, Ignite, Chill, Shock, Inflict), that ailment deals double damage per round
- If the attack causes Stun, the base stun duration is increased by 1 round
- When supporting a Greater skill, the damage dice from the Ruthless Blow are rolled with advantage (roll twice, take higher)

The Ruthless counter persists between combats and only resets on a long rest.
